Stress is a natural part of life, especially during exams or high-pressur situation. However, it can be manageble

1.Practice deep breathing exercises to calm your nervous system.
2.Maintain a consistent sleep schedule to improve your cognitive performence.
3.Engage in regular physical activity like walking, yoga or cycling.
4.Eat balanced meals and avoid excessive caffeine or sugar.
5.Use time management tools like planner or digital calenders to reduce overwhelm.
6.talk to a friend, teacher or mental health professional when needed

There habits can help reduce stress level and improve overall mental health.
